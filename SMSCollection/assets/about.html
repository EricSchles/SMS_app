Android SMS Collection App

Version: 1.0.3
Author: Tao Chen

This application allows you to automatically submit SMS messages on your Android device to Gmail as a draft email. We have not collected the messages in draft mail yet, so you need to send the email to us to make us collect your messages successfully. Our project aims to collect SMS messages and build a public research corpus. Our App is a modified version of an open-source App SMS Backup.
